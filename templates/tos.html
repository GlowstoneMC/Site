What's this all about?

The people behind this site are consumers - just like you. We also hate corporate-speak and overly-complicated terms with hidden motivations, just like you.

As a result, we've decided to put together a simple terms of service and information on all the data we collect on you and how it's used, as well as ways you can opt-out of each of these things.

The only thing we ask of you is to check back here every so often. As we don't harvest email addresses or other forms of contact, we're unable to notify you of changes to this page. While we reserve the right to edit our terms at any time, you may check back here whenever you like and the date we last updated this page will be displayed above.

Remember, by using this site, you are agreeing to our terms. If you don't agree with them, then we must unfortunately request that you do not use our services.

## How we collect data and how to opt-out
  • Top

Data collection

How data is collected

  • Google Analytics, including..
    • Pages visited on our site
    • Links taken to get to our site
    • Browser and OS information
    • Duration of your visit
  • Ultros bot-specific metrics collection, including..
    • Enabled plugins and protocol types
    • Installed packages
    • System info, such as total RAM, CPU, OS and Python version
    • Exceptions and errors that occur in the code
  • Webserver logs, including..
    • IP addresses and pages visited
    • Browser information
    • Site errors
  • Top

How to opt-out

  • Google Analytics:
    • The Google Analytics opt-out browser extension
    • Editing your HOSTS file
    • This CNET article on preventing Google tracking
  • Ultros bot-specific metrics collection:
    • Disabling your metrics - edit your settings.yml file and set metrics to off. This will not remove your data from our servers, but it will prevent any more data from being sent.
    • Destroying your metrics - edit your settings.yml file and set metrics to destroy. This will remove all of your bot-specific metrics from our servers, and it will prevent any more data from being sent.
  • Webserver logs:
    • Unfortunately, we need these logs for troubleshooting reasons. They may be removed from time to time as they get too large, though.
## What happens to the data
  • Top

Data usage policy

All data collected is strictly for our own use, however, we understand (and regret) that Google will use analytics data for their own purposes as well. Unfortunately, we aren't able to use server-side analytics as we lack the hardware to do so.

If you'd like to disable Google Analytics tracking, please see the section above.

Things that the data is used for:

  • Gauging interest in updates, packages and site reception.
  • Tracking the popularity of our project.
  • Public metrics display of bot version, system and usage information.
  • Popularity comparison to competing projects

Things that the data is NOT used for:

  • Sale to third-parties. We will never do this.
  • Advertising. We will never do this, either.
  • Espionage. We're not the NSA.

Questions? Please join us on IRC by clicking on the speech bubble icon at the very top-right of the page.

## Behavior on IRC
  • Top

Behavior on IRC

In addition to the above, the following rules should be obeyed on our IRC channels. Remember, these are guidelines, not concrete laws, and we reserve the right to punish users that break the rules in ways that are open to interpretation.

  • Follow the EsperNet network charter
  • Do not spam. This includes (but is not limited to) the following..
    • Advertising of any kind.
    • Derailing discussions about Ultros, or Ultros support.
      • This includes discussions and posting links that aren't related to Ultros, while discussions that are related to Ultros are in progress.
      • General chat is fine in all other cases, however.
    • Joining your own bots to the channel. We already have one!
    • Illegal discussions - for example, discussing where to pirate software.
    • Conversations and arguments of an intimate nature. Get a room, please!
  • No personal attacks or discrimination, or channel attacks. Leave your spambots, racism and bigotry at the door, please.
  • Bring your common sense with you into the channel, and don't be a dick - or else!
