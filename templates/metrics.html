## Metrics!
## Introduction to metrics

Metrics

We collect various anonymous metrics from running Ultros bots automatically, in the interests of curiosity and research, and for keeping track of popularity, interest and receptiveness to updates.

Metrics are collected anonymously and are easy to opt-out of. Each instance of Ultros gets a UUID, which is not public, and is the only way to identify a bot on the system. No IP addresses are collected, however they may appear in the server logs.

If you're running a bot and would like to disable metrics, simply open data/metrics.yml and change enable: true to enable: false. Please don't remove the UUID - we need it to delete our stored metrics for your bot. Please also note that bots with disabled metrics will still count towards the "total bots seen" count.

## General metrics

General

These are general metrics, which show some simple counts. Metrics are collected every ten minutes, which allows us to discern which bots are online. If a bot hasn't been seen for two weeks, then it's not considered "active" until it's seen again.
